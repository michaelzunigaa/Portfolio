About Me

I was born and raised in Tempe, Arizona. In 2016, I decided to moved away from the sunny desert to the beautiful emerald city of Seattle, Washington. Besides looking for a change in scenery, I was drawn to Seattle as it is one of the largest tech cities in the US. Growing up as a gamer, it had always been a dream of mine to find a job within that field and I knew moving here would be able to open those doors. In my free time, I love to watch superhero movies, attend events like PAX and Comicon, and challenge my friends to a round of Street Fighter. Currently, I live in West Seattle with my wife and our beloved tabby cat. I have a part-time job working at Chipotle to help pay for school but am actively looking for better opportunities for me and my family's future.
